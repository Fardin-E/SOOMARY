About us


  • A web application designed to make keeping up with the news easier.
  • Minimalistic web application to keep the focus on productivity.
  • Do you want to read the news but do not have enough time to go through the article? SOOMMARY was built for you.
  • You do not want to just read the article heading. Sometimes it is very misleading to make people click. If you really want to know more about the news but you lack the time, SOOMMARY is excellent to summarize the ongoings of the world to small digestable bits.
  • Keep track of every article you have summarized.
